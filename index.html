Start Breaking My Heart-Caribou/Manitoba

/>

Start Breaking my Heart is Dan Snaith's debut album. Released under the name "Manitoba", Snaith would soon take his more famous name, "Caribou", as a result of a lawsuit. This IDM/electronic record is nearly all instrumental and features many samples of live recordings. It's hard to describe, so I would just suggest listening to it. Do yourself a favor.
